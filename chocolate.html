Fudge is one of those things that is so rich and creamy that you can barely eat more than 3 squares. These desserts come in varieties of flavors and textures. This tends to be more on the expensive side since it’s a pretty uncommon thing to sell around in basic stores. Yeah, there are store-bought fudge like at Walmart and Fry’s, but homemade fudge in family-owned shops literally makes the best fudge.
