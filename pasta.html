One thing with pasta is that it sits in your stomach like a rock once you eat it. Pasta is very heavy in carbs and calories, but it tastes so good, so like I really don’t care about it. Spaghetti is really popular in the US. Usually, it has red sauce, long, skinny tube-like noodles with parmesan cheese on top. Then there’s other types of pasta as well with different sauces, and items like shrimp and chicken. Some restaurants and people put this green stuff on top, it’s sort of like little green leaves that give it an extra visual appeal and slightly -seasoned taste. I think it makes it look too veggy which takes away the amazing appeal of food all together.
